About

NOEMA was developed to address a fundamental constraint in signal systems: raw data is generated faster than it can be stored or transmitted.

In radar, RF, and sensor pipelines, this leads to irreversible data loss. Once discarded, raw signal captures cannot be reconstructed or reanalyzed.

NOEMA enables full data retention without changing infrastructure, through adaptive, lossless compression with bit-exact reconstruction.

The system operates directly on raw signal samples, adapting to their statistical structure regardless of encoding or representation.

Design Principles
No Approximation

No lossy stages. No reconstruction from models. Every bit is preserved.

Bit-Exact Reconstruction

Decoded output matches the original signal exactly. Verified deterministically.

No Pipeline Changes

Integrates without modifying formats, workflows, or downstream systems.

Deployment Flexibility

Runs on-premises, air-gapped, or in private cloud environments.

Context

NOEMA was developed in environments where storage, ingest, and bandwidth constraints directly limit what can be preserved.

The system is designed for pipelines where fidelity, reproducibility, and long-term access to raw signal data are non-negotiable.
