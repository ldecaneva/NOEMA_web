Who it's for

Designed for the most demanding signal pipelines.

Raw signal data is generated faster than it can be stored or transmitted. The data discarded under today's constraints is the data that cannot be recovered tomorrow.

Autonomous Vehicles
AV test fleets generate terabytes of raw radar data daily.

Storing everything is expensive; discarding any means losing edge cases. NOEMA compresses raw sensor streams losslessly, preserving every bit while reducing the storage footprint.

Satellite and SAR
Downlink windows are measured in minutes. What does not fit is gone.

NOEMA compresses SAR and telemetry data at the source, on the ground, and between relay stations. More science through the same link.

Defense and SIGINT
At the tactical edge, storage is constrained and connectivity is intermittent.

NOEMA compresses at the point of collection. Air-gappable, ARM-compatible, bit-exact. Keep every capture. Replay any signal later.

First principles

Every signal you discard is information you cannot recover.

You cannot retrain on data you did not keep.

Once raw signal captures are deleted, they are gone. The models you run tomorrow depend on what you store today.

You cannot reproduce experiments without the original signal.

Bit-exact reconstruction means any experiment, any analysis, any algorithm can be run against the original capture at any future point.

Lossless means nothing is approximated. Ever.

NOEMA does not trade fidelity for compression ratio. Every bit of the original signal is preserved and verifiable on output.
